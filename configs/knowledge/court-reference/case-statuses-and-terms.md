# Case Statuses and Terminology

## Case Lifecycle (मुद्दा जीवनचक्र)

### Investigation Phase (अनुसन्धान चरण)
- **Filed/Registered (दर्ता)**: Case has been registered at Special Court
- **Investigation Ongoing**: CIAA is actively investigating
- **Investigation Complete**: CIAA has completed investigation, preparing charge sheet

### Trial Phase (मुद्दा चरण)
- **Charge Sheet Filed (अभियोग पत्र दर्ता)**: CIAA has filed formal charges
- **Sub Judice (विचाराधीन)**: Case is under trial/hearing
- **Stay/Pause (स्थगित)**: Proceedings temporarily halted

### Resolution Phase (टुङ्ग्याउने चरण)
- **Decided/Verdict Issued (फैसला भएको)**: Court has issued judgment
- **Conviction (सजाय)**: Accused found guilty
- **Acquittal (सफाइ)**: Accused found not guilty
- **Dismissed (खारेज)**: Case dismissed on procedural or other grounds

### Appeal Phase (पुनरावेदन चरण)
- **Under Appeal (पुनरावेदनमा)**: Case appealed to higher court
- **Final Decision (अन्तिम फैसला)**: Supreme Court final verdict issued

## Sentence Types (सजाय प्रकार)

- **कैद सजाय (Imprisonment)**: Jail sentence, expressed in years/months
- **जरिवाना (Fine)**: Monetary penalty, expressed in Nepali Rupees
- **जफत (Confiscation)**: Property seizure order
- **बिगो असुल (Recovery)**: Recovery of misappropriated amount
- **पदमुक्त (Dismissal)**: Removal from public office
- **भविष्यमा पद धारण गर्न रोक (Disqualification)**: Barred from future public office

## Date Conventions

- **Bikram Sambat (BS)**: Nepali calendar, approximately 56.7 years ahead of AD
- **Anno Domini (AD)**: Gregorian calendar
- Always use `convert_date` tool for accurate BS↔AD conversion
- Common month names in BS: Baisakh, Jestha, Ashadh, Shrawan, Bhadra, Ashwin, Kartik, Mangsir, Poush, Magh, Falgun, Chaitra
